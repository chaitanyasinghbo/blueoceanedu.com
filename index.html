Programs

Four entry points, one standard.

The work changes by grade level. The bar does not. Each track is designed around the student's age, current profile, and available runway.

Grades 8 to 10

Build the foundation before pressure arrives.

For families who want to use time well. We identify interests, subject choices, early projects, reading habits, and skill foundations while the student still has room to grow.

  • IB and IGCSE subject direction
  • Interest discovery through serious work
  • First academic and service projects
  • Language, argumentation, and confidence
Image space Early cohort session or student workshop

Grade 11

The year where the profile becomes visible.

Grade 11 is where raw potential has to become evidence. We build the university list, narrative direction, recommendation strategy, projects, competitions, and testing plan.

  • University and country strategy
  • Personal narrative positioning
  • Competition and research planning
  • Recommendation relationship planning
Image space Profile planning wall or mentor table

Grade 12

Application work with depth, precision, and calm.

For students entering the final cycle, we guide school lists, Common App, UCAS, personal statements, supplements, interviews, financial aid, waitlists, and decision planning.

  • Common App and UCAS execution
  • Essay strategy and editing
  • Interview preparation
  • Decision-day and waitlist support
Image space Essay review, interview prep, or family meeting

Graduate admissions

For MBA, Masters, MPA, LLM, and specialist programs.

We advise applicants targeting Harvard Business School, Wharton, HEC Paris, INSEAD, LBS, Harvard Kennedy School, Oxford Blavatnik, and other elite graduate programs.

  • Career story and positioning
  • School selection and application calendar
  • Essays, recommendations, and interviews
  • Scholarship and decision support

Blue Ocean Profile Architecture

Develop the student. Admissions follow.

01 / Academic Project Development

Build Intellectual Work That Belongs in Your Application

We help students develop serious academic projects — work that reflects genuine curiosity, depth, and initiative. Not a school assignment. Not a resume filler. Something original that stands as primary evidence of who they are intellectually.

02 / Competition Strategy

Compete Where Winning Carries Real Weight

We identify competitions, fellowships, and prizes in your child's domain where results move admissions committees. Then we build a preparation strategy so the outcome becomes a meaningful part of the application story.

03 / Community Impact

Create Service That Outlasts Your Involvement

Generic volunteering does not move selective admissions committees. We help students design genuine community initiatives that are meaningful, sustainable, and capable of growing beyond their time in school. Work that reflects character, not just participation.

04 / Unique Skill Development

Find Your Distinctive Edge and Take It as Far as It Can Go

Every student has a natural strength that most programs never develop properly. We identify what makes each student genuinely different — in performance, domain expertise, original thinking, or craft — and help them reach a level that is hard to ignore.

05 / Language and Argumentation

Learn to Think, Write, and Argue With Precision

The clearest signal of intellectual maturity is how someone reasons through a problem and defends a position. We train students to think, speak, and write with clarity — strengthening their essays, interviews, and academic work from the ground up.

06 / Admissions Strategy

Connect Your Profile to the Right Universities, Worldwide

Blue Ocean students do not apply and hope. We build a targeted university list, a clearly argued positioning, and an application architecture that connects every part of the student's profile to a coherent story across the US, UK, Singapore, and beyond.

07 / Ongoing Mentorship

Work 1-1 With Our Founder-Led Team

Every student works directly with Dr. Sanjay Kumar and a team of mentors from Harvard, MIT, and the world's top universities. Weekly sessions drive progress. Monthly strategy meetings with Dr. Sanjay keep the full profile architecture sharp.

Right fit

This is selective by design.

The model works only when the family, student, counselor, and mentor all take the work seriously.

This program is for you if...

  • Your child is hardworking, ambitious, coachable, and willing to do the work.
  • You are focused on long-term development, not only the application.
  • You understand that elite outcomes require time, depth, and consistent execution.
  • You want the genuine attention of a founder-led team, not a junior counselor.

This program is not for you if...

  • You are looking for shortcuts or guaranteed admissions.
  • You want a done-for-you application service where the student does not grow.
  • You are looking for cheap counseling or a volume-based agency.
  • Your child is not serious about education, career, or future.
