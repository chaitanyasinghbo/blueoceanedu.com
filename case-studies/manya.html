The starting point

A strong student needed a file with one clear center.

Manya's target schools required more than grades and ambition. Blue Ocean's work was to help the application read as a consistent record of development: the right academic choices, a believable story, and evidence that held together across every part of the file.

The work

Profile architecture before application polish.

  • Audited academic and extracurricular material for strength, fit, and repetition.
  • Built a university strategy that protected ambition while keeping each application specific.
  • Structured essays around evidence, not inflated claims.
  • Aligned recommendations and final submissions around the same student narrative.

The outcome

Brown and Carnegie Mellon.

Manya earned offers from Brown and Carnegie Mellon, with approximately Rs 1.5 Cr in scholarship support recorded in Blue Ocean's internal outcomes list.
