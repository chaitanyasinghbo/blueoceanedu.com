The starting point

Two admissions systems, one student.

Anushka's file had to work for universities with different expectations. The task was to preserve one serious academic identity while adapting the strategy, writing, and evidence to each admissions context.

The work

Clarity across countries.

  • Built a common academic thread that could hold across both systems.
  • Strengthened writing for fit, argument, and intellectual maturity.
  • Kept final materials precise so the file did not split into two unrelated stories.

Columbia and Cambridge.

Anushka earned offers from Columbia and Cambridge, with approximately Rs 88L in scholarship support recorded in Blue Ocean's internal outcomes list.
