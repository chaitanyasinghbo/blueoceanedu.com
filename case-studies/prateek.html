The starting point

Harvard required evidence, not volume.

For Prateek, the challenge was not to make the file louder. It was to make the strongest parts of the student unmistakable: academic seriousness, intellectual range, and a reason for Harvard to believe the work would continue on campus.

The work

A file built for the closest read.

  • Clarified the central application argument before drafting essays.
  • Pressure-tested activities and evidence against Harvard-level scrutiny.
  • Reworked essay structure so the writing sounded like the student, not a consultant.
  • Aligned final materials around specificity, restraint, and proof.

The outcome

Harvard.

Prateek earned admission to Harvard, with approximately Rs 95L in scholarship support recorded in Blue Ocean's internal outcomes list.
